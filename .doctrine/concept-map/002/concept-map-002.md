# Concept Map: Workflow

Models the core doctrine workflow at conceptual altitude for a new user — the
mental models and vocabulary needed to use doctrine effectively. It does not
detail CLI command shapes or dispatch internals; it relates doctrine's entities
and common verbs to the jobs-to-be-done and the outcomes they produce.

## How to read it

The map has eight clusters, each a mental model:

### 1. Entry — route before you act
Substantive work is gated by the *Routing Gate* (`/route`), which chooses the
*governing skill* before any file is touched or command run. Its authority is the
*Boot Snapshot* (regenerated by the *Boot Command*, `doctrine boot`), and it leans
on *Memory* (retrieve instead of assume). The gate enforces *No Code Without
Approved Plan* — the load-bearing rule the whole lifecycle exists to uphold. The
*governing skill* wraps the *Doctrine CLI* (skills are process; the CLI is the
source of truth for command shapes).

### 2. Origins — where work comes from
Three origin paths: direct *User Intent* becomes a *Slice*; an *Idea or Problem*
is captured as a *Backlog Item* in the *Backlog* and promoted to a slice when
scoped; external issues arrive from GitHub (see cluster 8). Separately, the
*Dependency Graph* drives *Survey*, which *ranks* the *Eligible Entity*; *Next*
*filters to* the *Actionable Entity* (eligible **and** unblocked) — doctrine
tells you what to work on next, and that re-enters the routing gate. The graph
itself is authored by the relating-entities verbs (cluster 7).

### 3. The slice lifecycle — an explicit state machine
`Proposed → Design → Plan → Ready → Started → Audit → Reconcile → Done`, with
`Design` stress-tested by *Inquisition* before it locks, and `Design` producing
the slice's *Design Document*. Back-edges are predicate-driven (human/skill
judgement, not verb-enforced): an *Audit Finding* may return the slice to
`Started`; `Reconcile` may return to `Audit` or *escalate to* `Design` when the
spec/governance model itself is inadequate. A slice may be abandoned. Inside
`Started`, a *Phase* is *planned via* its *Phase Plan*, *built through* the *TDD
Loop*, and *verified against* its *Entry & Exit Criteria*.

### 4. Human gates & the conduct axis
The two load-bearing human gates (ADR-009) are `Ready` (approved plan — the "no
code without approved plan" handoff) and `Reconcile` (closure). `Done` is
*reached from* reconcile, not a gate itself — this corrects a common misreading.
Orthogonal to *what* the lifecycle is, the *Conduct Axis* declares *how* it is
conducted: a *Conduct Actor* ∈ {agent, self, peer, team} and an *Autonomy* ∈
{auto, draft, gate}. Defaults bake `Ready` and `Reconcile` as gates; the axis is
advisory in v1 (surfaced, not enforced).

### 5. Execution postures during Started
Work is conducted as *Solo* (driven by a *Single Agent*) or *Dispatch*
(isolated via *Worktree Forks*, orchestrated by the *Orchestrator*). The map
stops there — dispatch internals (workers, deltas, integration topology) are out
of scope for this high-level view.

### 6. Specifications & the truth model — the doctrine differentiator
Specs (*PRD*, *Tech Spec* descending from a PRD, *Requirement* descending from a
spec) own **normative truth**; *Coverage* and *Audit Findings* record **observed
truth**. Findings live in the *Review Ledger* (the RV kind) — the same substrate
used by *Inquisition* and *Audit*. When normative and observed diverge, that is
*Drift*, reconciled **explicitly by Reconcile — never by precedence** (the split
from spec-driver). *Reconcile* is the sole author of *Reconciled Status* and
applies exactly one *Reconcile Move* per requirement. A slice is governed by its
specs; specs are durable truth reconciled after the fact, not edited by
precedence.

### 7. Relating entities — the graph substrate
The dependency graph that drives *Survey/Next* is authored by three edge verbs:
*Needs* (a *Blocking Prerequisite*), *After* (a *Soft Sequence*), and *Link* (a
*Relation Edge*). All three feed the *Dependency Graph*. This is how doctrine
knows what blocks what, and what to do next.

### 8. Membership, mid-flight support, and external interaction
- **Membership — each kind answers a different question.** *Backlog Item* → what
  to do? · *ADR* → what was decided? · *Knowledge Record* → what do we know? ·
  *Memory* → what to retrieve? This is the intake test: work, decision, durable
  fact, or scoped knowledge — pick the right kind, don't conflate them. (Memory
  is scoped knowledge you retrieve; Knowledge Records are authored durable facts
  — distinct kinds, not the same thing.)
- **Mid-flight support & conduct postures.** *Consult* resolves a *Tradeoff*
  (don't improvise past an obstacle); *Canon* surfaces *Governance*; *Record
  Memory* enriches *Memory* and *Retrieve Memory* surfaces it (the capture/
  retrieve cycle); *Notes* records *Progress*; *Handover* transfers *Context* to
  a fresh agent. *Pairing* and *Walkthrough* are *Conduct Postures* that overlay
  the governing skill at any stage — they layer on, they don't replace it.
- **External interaction.** *GitHub Issues* feed the backlog; a *GitHub PR* is
  reviewed by a *Peer*; a *Team* coordinates *Dispatch*. Peers and teams are
  conduct roles on the lifecycle, not separate states.

## What was refined from the earlier draft
- "Router" → *Routing Gate* (the `/route` skill/concept, not a component).
- Corrected the human gates: `Ready` and `Reconcile`, not `Done` (per ADR-009).
- Added the **truth model** — the core differentiator (normative vs observed,
  drift, reconcile-never-by-precedence, reconciled status, reconcile move) —
  entirely missing before.
- Added back-edges and the `Reconcile → Design` model-gap escalation; the
  structural closure seam (`audit → reconcile → done`) is no longer flattened
  into uniform "advances to".
- Added the *Conduct Axis* (actor/autonomy), *Review Ledger* as the finding
  substrate, *Coverage*, *Revision* (REV change-axis), *Supersede*, the
  dependency-driven *Survey/Eligible/Next/Actionable* prioritisation, the
  relating-entities verbs (*needs/after/link*), *Phase Plan* + *Entry & Exit
  Criteria*, the *conduct postures* (pairing/walkthrough), *Notes*, and the
  retrieve half of the memory cycle.
- Deconflated *Memory* (scoped, retrieve) from *Knowledge Record* (authored
  durable fact).
- Trimmed dispatch internals per scope (postures and isolation only).
